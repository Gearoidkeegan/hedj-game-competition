Competition Privacy Notice
Plain English summary: We collect your name, email, and company solely to run this competition and contact you if you win. We keep it for 90 days then delete it. You can opt in separately if you'd like to hear from us after the competition closes.

WHO WE ARE

This competition is run by Hedj Financial Services Ltd, a treasury risk management firm based in Ireland. We are the data controller for information collected through this competition. You can contact us at info@hedj.eu.

WHAT WE COLLECT

When you register to compete, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your company name
  • Your game scores and results

We do not collect payment information, identification documents, or any sensitive personal data.

WHY WE COLLECT IT

Your information is used solely to administer this competition — to verify entries, maintain the leaderboard, and contact you if you are a prize winner. We have no other purpose for collecting it.

OPTIONAL: FOLLOW-UP CONTACT

During registration you can choose to opt in to receiving follow-up communications from Hedj after the competition closes. This is entirely optional and has no bearing on your competition entry or eligibility for prizes.

If you opt in, we may contact you to share information about Hedj's treasury risk management services. You can withdraw this consent at any time by emailing info@hedj.eu.

HOW LONG WE KEEP IT

All competition registration data — including your name, email, and company — will be permanently deleted within 90 days of the competition closing date. Game scores and leaderboard results may be retained in anonymised form beyond this period.

WHO WE SHARE IT WITH

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is stored securely on AWS infrastructure within the EU. Access is restricted to authorised Hedj staff only.

YOUR RIGHTS

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data at any time
  • Withdraw your consent to optional follow-up contact
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission (Ireland) at dataprotection.ie

To exercise any of these rights, email info@hedj.eu with the subject line "Competition Data Request". We will respond within 30 days.
